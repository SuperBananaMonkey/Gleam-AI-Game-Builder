Welcome to Gleam AI

Gleam is a beginner-friendly AI game builder. You can generate games directly in your browser, no coding required. Perfect for anyone who wants to create and play their own games instantly!

Click on the "Create" tab to start building your game now!

Meet the Gleam Team

The Gleam Team is dedicated to making game creation accessible for everyone. Our mission is to combine AI with intuitive tools so that anyone can bring their game ideas to life.

Help

How to use Gleam

  1. Go to the "Create" tab.
  2. Generate a new game using the AI builder.
  3. Play and test your game instantly in your browser.
  4. Share your creation with friends!

Tips

  • Start small — simple games are easier to iterate.
  • Experiment with different AI prompts for unique gameplay.
  • Check your game in multiple browsers for compatibility.
